History


Barangay Paule 1 traces its roots to the early settlement of indigenous communities in the region. Established as a vibrant community hub, it has evolved over time, witnessing socioeconomic growth and cultural diversity.
Today, Barangay Paule 1 stands as a testament to resilience, blending tradition with modernity while fostering a strong sense of community among its residents.
